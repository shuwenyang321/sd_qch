About Us

Introducing SD-QCH

SD-QCH (San Diego Queer Community Hub) is the nonprofit home for emerging founders, creative builders, and entrepreneurial allies ready to launch ventures rooted in community impact.

We curate modern, design-forward programming that pairs strategic expertise with the care of a supportive collective—because building a business should feel energizing, inclusive, and connected.

Why we exist

San Diego needed a dedicated space where queer and allied founders could access mentorship, capital knowledge, and peer accountability without compromising authenticity.

How we activate

Experiential labs, curated salons, outdoor resets, and digital cohorts give members space to test ideas, refine strategy, and build traction with confidence.

Mission

We create connection, clarity, and courage for new entrepreneurs.

Our collective bridges knowledge gaps, sparks collaboration, and showcases the diverse voices leading the next wave of sustainable, values-led businesses across the San Diego region.

Community First

We nurture a supportive network where mentorship, peer exchange, and mutual wins power entrepreneurial journeys.

Learning in Motion

Our events blend pragmatic workshops, expert panels, and hands-on labs tailored to real founder challenges.

Inclusive Growth

We champion access for underrepresented founders, centering queer, BIPOC, and gender diverse voices in business.
