About Us

Introducing San Diego 启创汇

SD-QCH (San Diego 启创汇) is an innovation collective based in San Diego that catalyzes new ventures with founders, investors, and operators joining from everywhere.

We curate bilingual programming—online and in-person—that pairs real-world market insight with grassroots community support—because launching from here or afar should feel confident, well-resourced, and collaborative.

Why we exist

San Diego needed a dedicated platform that stays locally grounded while founders everywhere access mentorship, capital knowledge, and peer accountability with language and cultural nuance.

How we activate

Market briefings, investor salons, immersion tours, and digital cohorts give members space to test ideas, localize strategy, and build traction with confidence.

Mission

We create connection, clarity, and courage for founders everywhere.

Our collective bridges knowledge gaps, sparks collaboration, and showcases the multilingual voices shaping the next wave of sustainable, community-minded businesses across San Diego and beyond.

Community First

We nurture a supportive network where mentors, alumni, and partners share resources that power entrepreneurial journeys across neighborhoods and time zones.

Learning in Motion

Our events blend pragmatic workshops, expert panels, and hands-on labs tailored to real challenges in scaling products and partnerships.

Inclusive Mindset

We champion multilingual access and cultural fluency so founders can negotiate partnerships, attract capital, and tell their story with confidence—wherever they call home.
